# Glossary

_Domain terms used in this project. Append-only with dated edits._

| Term | Definition | Added |
|---|---|---|
| Account | A money container: bank account, cash wallet, PayPal, dorm cashier, etc. | 2026-07-03 |
| Transaction | Any money movement: expense, income, or transfer between accounts. | 2026-07-03 |
| Transfer | A transaction that moves money from one Account to another (no net change). | 2026-07-03 |
| Loan | Money lent to or borrowed from a friend; tracked until settled. | 2026-07-03 |
